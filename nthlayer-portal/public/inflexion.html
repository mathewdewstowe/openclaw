How it works

Five stages. One executive report.

Stage 01 Frame Define the decision

What’s changed, what’s at stake, and what winning looks like. Every constraint made explicit before analysis begins.

Stage 02 Diagnose Establish what is true

Where do you actually stand? A structured fact base across product, stack, workflows, and team. Every claim sourced and scored.

Stage 03 Decide Choose the direction

Real options surfaced — including doing nothing. Each pressure-tested against what must be true for it to work.
