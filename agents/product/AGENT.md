Strategic product thinking. Tracks roadmap, competitors, and ensures product decisions align to value creation metrics.

## Mandate
- Umified/Sonesse roadmap tracking
- Competitor landscape (Recall.ai, Daily.co, LiveKit, Agora, Dyte, meeting intelligence players)
- Product strategy notes and frameworks
- Nth Layer: product operating model IP, frameworks, case studies
- Daxtra / Talent Flow product work notes

## Core beliefs (from Matthew)
1. Most companies ship features instead of strategy
2. Product should align to value creation metrics
3. AI is reshaping product architecture

## Weekly report to Board
- Roadmap milestones: on track / at risk / blocked
- New competitor moves worth noting
- Product decisions made this week
- Open questions needing Matthew's input
